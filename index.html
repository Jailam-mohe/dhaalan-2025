Enhanced Services

Revolutionary Service Offerings

Comprehensive integration of educational, employment, and skills development services through our digital platform ecosystem

🎓 Educational Services

HEMS Portal Integration

Comprehensive Higher Education Management System enabling on-the-spot processing of First Degree Scheme approvals and international applications.

Live Degree Approvals

Eligible students receive immediate First Degree Scheme approval and secure university admissions on-site.

International University Hub

Foreign universities offering exclusive scholarships and on-the-spot admissions through HEMS Portal integration.

Student Loan Center

Complete loan utilization processing, agreement signing, and university placement facilitation during the event.

MQA Recognition Desk

Instant course recognition services ensuring international qualifications are validated immediately.

SAILS Training Programs

Access to Tuition Free Certificate 3 and 4 programs through local institutions for practical skills development.

💼 Employment Solutions

National Careers Portal

Live tracking and management of job applications with real-time updates and comprehensive employment statistics. Facilitates apprenticeship and internship opportunities through various employers.

SAILS Apprenticeship Programs

SAILS Project facilitated apprenticeship opportunities across various industries and skill levels, connecting students with employers for practical experience.

Mobile App Integration

Live mobile app functionality streamlining job applications, interview scheduling, and offer management.

Project Timeline

Event Timeline & Milestones

Strategic roadmap from project initiation to post-event analysis, ensuring seamless digital platform integration and successful execution

June 1, 2025

Project Launch & Digital Integration

Form steering committee, finalize venue, launch official website, integrate HEMS Portal and National Careers Service Portal, and establish NCIT support framework.

June 15, 2025

Sponsorship & Partnership Drive

Begin corporate outreach, international university negotiations, MNSDA collaboration agreements, and MQA partnership establishment.

July 1, 2025

Marketing Campaign & System Testing

Roll out comprehensive marketing strategy, begin HEMS Portal stress testing, National Careers Portal integration testing, and mobile app beta releases.

August 1, 2025

Exhibitor & Educational Partner Finalization

Complete exhibitor participation, finalize international university partnerships, secure SAILS training provider agreements, and establish scholarship packages.

September 1, 2025

Technology Integration & Staff Training

Deploy mobile apps, complete HEMS and National Careers Portal integration, train NCIT support staff, and conduct system-wide testing.

October 1, 2025

Final Preparations & System Deployment

Venue setup, final system deployment, staff comprehensive training, pre-event registration campaigns, and logistics coordination.

November 6-8, 2025

Event Execution & Live Monitoring

Three-day expo with real-time HEMS Portal processing, live National Careers Portal tracking, NCIT support services, and comprehensive success monitoring.

November 15, 2025

Post-Event Analysis & Follow-up

Comprehensive evaluation, digital platform performance analysis, stakeholder feedback collection, and long-term impact assessment reporting.
